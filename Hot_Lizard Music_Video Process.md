## Stylized shading method

Remove/add more realism than there was before.

It is composed of multiple components. Starting with the principled BSDF for most realism and detail and slowly stripped of everything unnecessary through the adage of extra nodes and shader layers, for stylization purposes. Inspiration came from this video

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3WLjjwGgM-8)

----

The point currently is to have a stylized blobby wet brain that has the style of can be deduced to be made by or have a relation to AI technologys.

What this specifically is, is the sludge that the brain turns into (can be referenced towards and interpreted in the song). 
Because the end result of AI Image and video generation is a design that devolves into, functions as and or somewhat looks like sludge. In other words "Slop".

Its a combination of everything, turning it into a vicious liquid metaphorically.
